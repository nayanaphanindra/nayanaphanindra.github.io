About

I design learning that transfers — not learning that ticks a box.

With roots in K–12 education and national-level teacher training at NCERT, I bring a deep understanding of how people learn — and how to design for it intentionally.

I specialise in building scaffolded, learner-centric experiences: from needs analysis and curriculum design to facilitation and evaluation. Whether the learner is a Grade 8 student or a corporate professional, the goal is the same — meaningful transfer, not just completion.
